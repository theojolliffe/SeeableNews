How coronavirus spread through England's care homes

At least 13,500 deaths — nearly 1 in 3 — have occurred in British care homes in the United Kingdom. Deaths attributed to other causes have also risen sharply over the same period — so much so that the homes have 26,000 more deaths than is typical, a concept known as “excess deaths.”
